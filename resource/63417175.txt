The Chinese authorities said the station offered a service to Chinese citizens in Ireland including the renewal of driving licences.', 'The Chinese government has been accused of establishing similar facilities across Europe, including two in London and  in the Netherlands. ', 'Dutch media found evidence that the "overseas service stations", which promise to provide diplomatic services, are being used to try to silence Chinese dissidents in Europe.', 'A spokeswoman for the Dutch foreign ministry said the existence of the unofficial police outposts was illegal, but the Chinese foreign ministry rejected the allegations.', 'Scottish Green MSP Ross Greer questioned Nicola Sturgeon on the <a href="https://safeguarddefenders.com/sites/default/files/pdf/110%20Overseas%20(3).pdf" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Chinese Transnational Policing Gone Wild</a> report during FMQs in the Scottish Parliament.
