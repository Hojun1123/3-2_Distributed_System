['<b class="ssrcss-hmf8ql-BoldText e5tfeyi3">A former councillor and cabinet member has had his conviction for Covid fraud overturned.</b>', 'Harman Banger and wife Neena Kumari, of Bilston Road, Wolverhampton, had been found guilty of fraud by false representation in December.', 'The charge stemmed from an application for a £10,000 small business loan grant to run a takeaway site during the pandemic.', 'A judge at Birmingham Crown Court overturned the conviction on Thursday.', 'Mr Banger, 41, who had sat on Wolverhampton City Council, was suspended by Labour when allegations emerged.
