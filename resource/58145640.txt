", '"I\'m holding a huge respect for what he and his team have built," said Mr Oestberg.', 'The chief executive of the German company added that the stake in Deliveroo was acquired "at [a] decent gross profit margin". Delivery Hero, he said, considered Deliveroo\'s stock to be "undervalued", due to being "oversold at IPO".', 'Online food delivery platforms have seen a jump in popularity due to coronavirus lockdowns, but despite rising sales, neither Delivery Hero nor Deliveroo are yet to make a profit.
