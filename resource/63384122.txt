', 'Joel is the 41st Blue Peter presenter and will co-host alongside Richie Driss, Mwaksy Mudenda and Henry the dog.', 'His first live show airs on Friday 11 November on CBBC and BBC iPlayer at 17:00 GMT. ', 'Joel attended Bangor Grammar before going on to pursue his career in entertainment at age 18. ', '"Becoming the 41st Blue Peter presenter is a surreal - and dare I say it - magical experience," he said. ', '"I\'ve always loved making people smile with my magic and I want to do the same for the Blue Peter audience. ', '"They have high expectations and I\'ll do my best to live up to them, I can\'t wait to get started." ', 'Joel guest presented the show over summer 2022 when he taught some of his co-presenters magic tricks. ', 'His interest in magic started age five and he began performing at the age of 11. ', 'He is best known for his illusions on social media where he has a huge following, with over 16.5m on one platform alone.
