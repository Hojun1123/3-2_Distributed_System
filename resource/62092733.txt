', 'The River Ythan catchment in Aberdeenshire and the Firth of Tay have been put into moderate scarcity level due to very dry ground conditions and low river levels.', 'Areas around the River Irvine and River Ayr in Ayrshire are also now at the early warning stage, again due to low river levels.
