['<b class="ssrcss-hmf8ql-BoldText e5tfeyi3">Up to 20 council homes may be made available by a local authority as part of plans to house Ukrainian refugees if their original host agreements end. </b>', 'Only 23% of hosts in Cambridge said they were likely to continue beyond the initial 12 month period of the <a href="https://www.gov.uk/register-interest-homes-ukraine" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0"> Homes for Ukraine scheme</a>.', '<a href="https://www.cambridge.gov.uk/" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Cambridge City Council</a> is developing a homelessness prevention plan in case it is needed, a report said. ', 'There are 385 guests in 203 households in the city area, the report added.
