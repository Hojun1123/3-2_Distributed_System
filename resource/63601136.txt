", '"His remains could not be recovered, and there is no evidence that he was ever a prisoner of war," said the DPAA in a statement.', "Hanks's identification became possible after North Korea turned over 55 boxes containing the remains of American service members killed during the Korean War.", 'It came after a summit between then-President Donald Trump and North Korean leader Kim Jong-un in June 2018.
