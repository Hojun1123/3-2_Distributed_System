She has also not used her car since the start of the school holidays.', 'She explained how she had to tell her children they could not afford to go to friends\' birthday parties or do other things with them with "no spare money".', '"Unless you sit down and explain it, they think you are a mean mum," she said.', '"It\'s a vicious cycle and it\'s getting worse by the week."', 'Meanwhile, Mr Samani and wife Gita have run a Spar on Kings Fee in Wonastow, Monmouthshire, since 1987 and have survived several recessions but described recent cost rises as "unbelievable".', '"It\'s going to get even harder by next year because the recession is going to affect local people, especially working-class people," said Mr Samani, a father-of-three. ', 'With inflation now topping 10%, Mr Samani said he had no option but to raise prices.', '"I have to or I won\'t survive. I\'ve got overheads, bills and rates to pay, it\'s so much now that we can\'t afford not to do that," he said.', 'Mr Samani employs six people but he admits small businesses, such as his struggle against larger supermarkets.', "The shop's electricity bill has risen from £479 a month to £750 a month in the space of a year while National Insurance for his staff and business rates have risen.", 'After 35 years Mr Samani said he knew his customers personally, many of whom were living day-to-day.', '"They always talk about [costs going up], especially now with the electricity prices, the gas prices, petrol prices."', 'Meanwhile people in Treharris, near Merthyr Tydfil, are also feeling the pinch.', 'Eileen, 84, from Merthyr Vale, said she was concerned at how young families could cope.', '"I\'m worried about the grandchildren and great grandchildren coming into this world with the price of everything. How are they going to live?" ', 'Her friend Ann Tweedy, 75, said living on a pension and disability allowance was a struggle.', '"The food in the shops is going up a lot. Our electric and gas, that\'s what I\'m going to be very worried about… it\'s risen so much that we\'ll have to be very careful with our money," she said.', '"I\'m trying not to spend too much on other things.', '"At the moment we\'ve very lucky we can afford to do different things, but in the future we don\'t know how often we can come out with the prices going up."', 'She added: "It\'s going to be very hard for the youngsters, and one thing we don\'t want them to do is go to these loan sharks.', '"We\'ll all try and help each other.
