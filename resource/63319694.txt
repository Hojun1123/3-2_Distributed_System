', 'Meanwhile, Amina Harun, who lives in London with her husband and two children, says her experience of using a GP practice has been "appalling".', 'Her practice has had to rely on locums, she says, and the service is "impersonal".', '"I\'ve had terrible experience with my GP and still continue to have bad experiences," Amina says.', '"You can\'t call to book appointments and have to do everything via an online app.', '"This is not good for anyone that is vulnerable and unable to book via phone.', '"The phone lines are always busy or there\'s usually a long wait."', 'The committee called for a return of personal lists, so each patient is assigned an individual GP, alongside an expansion of GP training places.', 'By 2027, 80% of GP practices should be using personal lists, it said, and 100% by 2030.', 'The committee said the poorest areas, which tended to have the fewest doctors and highest levels of ill-health, needed more funding.', 'An analysis by the Nuffield Trust think tank earlier this year for BBC News found a <a href="https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/health-61598158" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">two-fold variation in the number of patients per GP</a> across different areas of England.', 'Prof Martin Marshall, who chairs the Royal College of GPs, said: "The report recognises what the college has been saying for many years -
