', 'A government body is consulting the public on how best to remember those who lost their lives to the virus. ', 'Cameron\'s dad, Neil, said local sites would help families "reflect and commemorate the loss of a loved one".', 'Mr Day, from Scunthorpe, said the site could involve plaques, bricks or stones engraved to remember those lost. ', '"We\'d like to see something regional because, up and down the country, families will have lost loved ones across every region," he said.
