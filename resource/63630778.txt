', "A documentary about her life is set to have its premiere next week in New York. She also has plans to publish a children's book in January. ", "In addition to Killing Me Softly - which was later covered by the Fugees - Flack is known for songs including The First Time Ever I Saw Your Face, which launched her to stardom after it was used in Clint Eastwood film Play Misty for Me, and Feel Like Makin' Love. ", 'Her condition - Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS), also known as Lou Gehrig\'s disease - "has made it impossible to sing and not easy to speak," her management said in a statement. ', '"But it will take a lot more than ALS to silence this icon," they said, adding that she "plans to stay active in her musical and creative pursuits".', "There is no known cure for ALS, which is caused by the death of the nerves that carry messages from the brain to people's muscles.
