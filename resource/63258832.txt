', '<a href="https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/m000c9fp" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Speaking to Zoe Ball on BBC Radio 2</a>, he said he was joined by "incredibly gifted" producers and musicians.
