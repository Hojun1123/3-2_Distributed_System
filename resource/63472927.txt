['<b class="ssrcss-hmf8ql-BoldText e5tfeyi3">New walking trails designed by local people are connecting four villages to a protected wetland reserve. </b>', 'The trails will link Earith, Bluntisham, Over and Needingworth in Cambridgeshire to RSPB Ouse Fen.', 'Mapping the routes has been part of the <a href="https://www.rspb.org.uk/our-work/conservation/projects/hanson-rspb-wetland-project/" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Hanson-RSPB Wetland Project</a> - a scheme to reclaim quarry land and expand the fen to 700 hectares by 2030.', "The RSPB's Jonathan Taylor said many villagers were unaware that the wetland was on their doorstep.", 'Residents took part in workshops to <a href="https://www.rspb.org.uk/reserves-and-events/reserves-a-z/ouse-fen/" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">advise on the routes</a>, pinpoint landmarks for walkers, and suggest ways to improve the habitat such as birdboxes and benches.', 'Mr Taylor said Ouse Fen has been undergoing a "significant restoration project" with Hanson UK, which runs a quarry at Needingworth.', "The company is gradually transferring quarry land back to nature, creating the UK's biggest reed bed covering 460 hectares, and expanding Ouse Fen to 700 hectares of wildlife-rich fenland habitat over the next eight years.", 'The last purchase of land in 2021 took the site to 300 hectares (about 420 football pitches).', '"At this time of year you\'d be able to see marsh harriers flying overhead with their distinctive V shape, or bearded tits pinging around in the reed bed," Mr Taylor said.', '"We opened a new car park last summer on the B1050 near Earith, but we realised that some local villagers weren\'t aware that the reserve\'s even there.', '"These maps are designed to bring people in without them having to drive to the new entrance."', 'RSPB project officer Charlotte Mulderrig said: "Walking and getting out into nature has proven physical and mental health benefits.
