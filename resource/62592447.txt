', 'Investigators found Flint, of Bidhams Crescent, Tadworth, Surrey, used details of an acquaintance fraudulently when setting up new eBay accounts.', 'When police searched his house, they found empty vaccine vials and receipts for packages posted to buyers.', "He was found guilty of theft at Staines Magistrates' Court, having previously pleaded guilty to fraud by false representation.
