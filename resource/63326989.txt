He\'s a little miracle."', 'Cassie and her partner Jack are full of praise for the friend who enabled them to have a baby.', '"She put her life on hold for us for a year", Jack said. "There\'s nothing we can do that\'ll match it."', '"She\'s just incredible," Cassie added.
