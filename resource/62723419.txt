', '"This places incredible strain on local wildlife and this is why we are moving to drought status.  ', '"We are prioritising our local operations to minimise impacts on the environment," added Mr Paul.', 'The EA said it will be monitoring the effects of the dry weather on rivers and responding to environmental emergencies, such as rescuing stranded fish.
