"Charlbi had a care and sensitivity that lifted her colleagues and the entire film crew.', '"The thought that she will not be by our side in the future makes me very sad."', "Dean's friend and fellow actress, The Vampire Diaries star Nina Dobrev, shared images of the two of them together.", '"An angel has gone to heaven far too soon," <a href="https://www.instagram.com/stories/nina/2916387577691145344/?hl=en" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">she posted</a>. "May heaven be all you ever imagined."', 'Writing in <a href="https://www.theguardian.com/film/2022/aug/30/charlbi-dean-was-a-true-star-in-the-making-her-loss-is-a-huge-one" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">The Guardian</a>, Peter Bradshaw said: "Charlbi Dean was a true star-in-the-making. I was so looking forward to where her career was going to go next."', 'Another critic, <a href="https://twitter.com/GuyLodge/status/1564659201523212290" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Guy Lodge</a>, called it "a massive shock", adding: "She\'s so witty and deadpan and clockwork-precise in Triangle of Sadness, and I was so excited to see a new South African talent on the international scene."', 'Playwright <a href="https://twitter.com/jeremyoharris/status/1564656817351053312" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Jeremy O Harris</a> described the late actress as a "true talent".
