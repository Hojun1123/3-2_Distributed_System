', 'Ms Honey-Jones said she was "refreshing the news feeds" while she travelled from her home in Swansea with her husband and daughter as they were aware of some flights being cancelled. ', '"It\'s the whole machine struggling and under pressure as we build out of the pandemic," he added.', 'Aviation analyst Alex Macheras said the UK was seeing some of the worst of the disruption, which he believes is down to the aviation sector as a whole being "incredibly short of staff".', '"We\'re seeing the after effects of all the decisions taken in the pandemic, meeting this early surge in summer demand. ', '"Tens of thousands of staff were let go because of pandemic and these companies are so short of staff. ', '"It\'s become the perfect storm.
