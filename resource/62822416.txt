', "The 12-week personalised programme will be trialled at gyms and Burton Albion's fitness suite, with participants being evaluated afterwards.", 'Dr Lewis Gough, senior lecturer in sport and exercise at the university who is leading the study, said: "We know that long Covid can have a seriously debilitating effect on fitness levels among people who previously didn\'t do much exercise, but also among those who led very healthy, active lifestyles.
