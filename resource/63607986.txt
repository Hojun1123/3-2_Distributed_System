', 'Staff earning less than £30,000 will receive a single payment of £600, with staff earning more getting less.', 'Buckingham Palace declined to comment. ', 'Other royal household staff will also receive a bonus, the BBC understands.', 'Those on between £30,000 and £40,000 will receive £400 in a single payment, and those who are paid between £40,000 and £45,000 will receive £350, the Sun reported.
