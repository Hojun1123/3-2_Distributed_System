Ye\'s recent comments and actions have been unacceptable, hateful and dangerous, and they violate the company\'s values of diversity and inclusion, mutual respect and fairness."', 'Instagram and Twitter suspended Ye\'s account in the days following his anti-Semitic remarks. Among various celebrities decrying Ye\'s anti-Semitic remarks were former \'Friends\' actor David Schwimmer, and Ye\'s former wife Kim Kardashian, a reality TV star who is currently studying to become a lawyer. ', '"Antisemitism, racism and hate in any form are inexcusable and not tolerated... We are partnering with organisations that combat hate and discrimination," Gap said on its Instagram account, @yeezyxgap.', 'Although Adidas put its relationship under review after Ye showed his "White Lives Matter" T-shirt, the company did not say the collection was the reason for the review.
