['<b class="ssrcss-hmf8ql-BoldText e5tfeyi3">Former Dragons\' Den star Hilary Devey has died aged 65.</b>', 'Devey, who grew up in Leeds, died after a long illness on Saturday night, her publicist Benjamin Webb said.', 'The Bolton-born entrepreneur, who founded the multimillion-pound freight distribution business Pall-Ex in the 1990s, died at her holiday home in Morocco.', 'Mr Webb said Devey was a "remarkable lady and an inspiration" who was "so proud" of her work on Dragons\' Den.', 'Hannah Wyatt, managing director for Factual Entertainment and Events at BBC Studios, said: "Hilary Devey will always hold a unique place in the hearts of Dragons\' Den viewers and the programme team.
