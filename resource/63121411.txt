', 'In Yorkshire &amp; the Humber, the RSPCA has responded to 2,830 abandoned animals this year, as of July. ', 'The second largest percent increase was seen in the East Midlands, with the charity responding to 2,144 animal abandonments so far this year - up 35%.', 'That is closely followed by the South East, where 2,751 animals have been abandoned, up 34%.
