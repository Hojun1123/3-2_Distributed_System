', 'The continued availability of free LFTs to symptomatic people "should make a difference" according to Dr Sir Frank Atherton, the chief medical officer for Wales. ', '"So when we\'re on a rise of community infection, when rates are actually going up, people are coming to more serious harm, that\'s the time to delay those changes.', '"And that\'s really why we have decided in Wales to continue with the testing for people who are symptomatic with coronavirus symptoms for a further month."', 'Self-isolation payments of £500 will end on 30 June, while the Covid-19 <a href="https://gov.wales/covid-19-statutory-sick-pay-enhancement-scheme" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">statutory sick pay enhancement scheme</a> will be extended until 31 August to support social care staff to stay away from work if they test positive.
