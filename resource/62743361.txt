But the cadets gave me a community and helped me find my place in my new country."', 'Col Owen, commandant of Yorkshire (North and West) ACF, said: "Ashanti Mai is a fine example of what young people can achieve when they put their mind to it and she has truly risen to the very highest pinnacle for a cadet.', '"Since joining the cadets she has embraced every opportunity given to her.
