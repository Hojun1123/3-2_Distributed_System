['<b class="ssrcss-hmf8ql-BoldText e5tfeyi3">Dyson has been fined over a million pounds after an employee was injured when a milling machine fell on him.</b>', 'The technology company has been ordered to pay £1.2m after pleading guilty to breaching health and safety laws. ', 'Health and Safety Executive (HSE) inspector, James Hole, said the incident "could have been fatal".', 'Dyson said it was "thankful" that the employee was "not more seriously hurt" in the incident at its factory in Malmesbury, Wiltshire.
