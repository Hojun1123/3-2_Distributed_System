", 'The proposed development at Minchens Lane - known as Bramley Frith - would have been operational for up to 40 years and span 85 hectares (210 acres) of agricultural land. ', 'Bramley Solar Ltd, the company behind the project, said previously the solar farm would have provided "reliable and urgently needed source of low carbon and renewable energy".  ', "Basingstoke and Deane Borough Council's planning officers had recommended that the plan got the go-ahead at a meeting on Wednesday. ", "But 11 of 12 councillors at the authority's development control committee voted against it. ", 'Ron Hussey, a Liberal Democrat councillor who was the only member in favour of the plan, said the decision left him "ashamed" of being a committee member.
