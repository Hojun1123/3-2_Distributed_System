', '"The adversity that we\'ve been through as a family has made me who I am."', 'Mr Roberts went on to get top grades in his A-levels while working two jobs to earn a place at the University of Bristol.', 'Although he "absolutely loved" studying in the city, his father\'s disease continued to loom large.', '"I knew things were really going to change - I did years of therapy with the university\'s mental health services in preparation," he said.', '"Two days before the final exam of my undergrad, my dad called me and said he didn\'t have much time left.
