', 'Police have registered a case, but they are yet to make any arrests.', 'In a statement issued on Wednesday, DCW Chairperson Swati Maliwal said the girl, a student of a government-run school in the capital, Delhi, had alleged that she was going to her classroom in July when she collided with two boys - both high school students at the same school. ', 'The girl said that she apologised to the boys, "but they started abusing her and took her inside a toilet, locked the door and allegedly raped her", the statement added. ', 'Ms Maliwal said the girl had also accused the school authorities "of covering up the matter". ', 'The school has maintained that the girl did not report the incident to the principal, but it has ordered an investigation.
