", 'The UK\'s singer Sam Ryder was <a href="https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/entertainment-arts-61452678" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">runner-up to Ukraine\'s Kalush Orchestra this May</a>.', 'A hotly-contested selection process saw 20 UK cities express an interest in hosting the show before that was narrowed down to seven, and now a final two.', 'Liverpool City Council and Liverpool City Region Combined Authority said they had <a href="https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-merseyside-62478794" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">worked together to produce a bid</a> which contained details about venues, transport links, funding and the city\'s past experience of hosting major events.', "If successful, Eurovision would be staged at the 11,000-capacity dockside M&amp;S Bank Arena, which stands next to a conference centre and close to the city centre's hotels and rail links.", 'Ms Anderson said hosting Eurovision "would write a brand-new chapter in our musical heritage", be a "huge boost to the local economy" and provide a "platform to sustain jobs well into the future".', 'She said "nobody throws a party like Liverpool" and while Glasgow was "a great city... there is no city anywhere in Europe which would make a more perfect home for Eurovision than Liverpool".
