', '"Sharing The Light is an exuberant event celebrating the city of Birmingham through an eclectic composition of dance and music and visuals."', 'The word Diwali comes from the Sanskrit word deepavali, meaning "rows of lighted lamps" - and houses, shops and public places are decorated with small oil lamps called diyas.', 'This year Diwali takes place over five days from 22-26 October with the main day of celebration on Monday 24 November.', 'Graham Callister, Director of Festivals at Birmingham Hippodrome, said: "We are thrilled to welcome the annual global Diwali celebrations to Birmingham through a mix of free, high-quality local South Asian-led and international arts, performances, installations and workshops that engage the city\'s communities, schools and families."', '<b class="ssrcss-hmf8ql-BoldText e5tfeyi3">Part of Friday\'s event was captured on the </b><a href="https://fb.watch/giQLgw45Kj/" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">BBC Midlands Facebook</a><b class="ssrcss-hmf8ql-BoldText e5tfeyi3"> page.</b>', '<i class="ssrcss-xbdn93-ItalicText e5tfeyi2">Follow BBC West Midlands on </i><a href="https://www.facebook.com/MidlandsBBC/" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Facebook</a><i class="ssrcss-xbdn93-ItalicText e5tfeyi2">, </i><a href="https://twitter.com/bbcmtd" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Twitter</a><i class="ssrcss-xbdn93-ItalicText e5tfeyi2"> and </i><a href="https://www.instagram.com/bbc_midlands/?hl=en" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Instagram</a><i class="ssrcss-xbdn93-ItalicText e5tfeyi2">.
