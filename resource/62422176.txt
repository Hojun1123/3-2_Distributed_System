Exxon Mobil\'s exploration of new oil &amp; gas".', 'One of the protesters read out a statement that said: "By funding fossil fuels, Barclays is making our climate more dangerous, which is killing people now."', 'The members of Extinction Rebellion in Reading were gathered outside HSBC on Broad Street on Monday for its reopening after a three-month refurbishment.', 'Along with fake police tape, protesters also displayed false HSBC advertising that said "today, you\'re \'on mute\', tomorrow you\'re on target for 3C global warming", according to <a href="https://ldrs.org.uk/" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">the Local Democracy Reporting Service</a>.', 'A Barclays spokesperson told the BBC: "We are determined to play our part in addressing the urgent and complex challenge of climate change."', 'They said: "We have a three-part strategy to turn that ambition into action: achieving net zero operations, reducing our financed emissions, and financing the transition."', 'The spokesperson added that the bank has set 2030 targets to reduce our financed emissions in four of the highest emitting sectors in our financing portfolio as well as investing billions in green financing and green start-ups.', 'A spokesperson for HSBC UK told the BBC: "We remain committed to supporting our customers in their transition to net zero.', '"HSBC has an important role in the transition to a more sustainable world."
