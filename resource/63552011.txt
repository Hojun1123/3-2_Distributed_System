No hearing for the appeal has been set at this stage.', '<a href="https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-53919624" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Tarrant was jailed to life without parole in 2020</a> for the murder and attempted murder of Muslim worshippers.', 'Prime Minister Jacinda Ardern said he was attempting to "revictimise people".', 'Asked about his appeal, Ms Ardern told reporters: "His is a name that shouldn\'t be repeated and I\'m going to apply that same rule in commenting on his attempts to revictimise people.
