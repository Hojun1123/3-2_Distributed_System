', 'Each year the United Nations hosts the global climate summit, called the Conference of Parties (COP), to get countries around the world to work together on tackling climate change. ', 'But according to Mr Rees, Bristol and other cities cannot rely on government funding to pay for the amount of public works needed to cut carbon emissions to net zero.
