$42.5bn according to Comscore, buoyed by films such as Avengers: Endgame and Frozen 2.', 'So far in 2022, films such as Jurassic World Dominion and Minions: The Rise of Gru have performed strongly.', 'But box office takings this year are down by around a third, or 32%, compared with 2019.', 'Cinema chains have also been facing tough competition from streaming services, which soared in popularity
