', 'Mortgage advisors have also expressed concerns for homeowners on fixed-rate mortgages. ', "Neil Butler, a mortgage advisor with Ocean Mortgages said he hadn't seen anything like the current situation in years. ", '"I\'ve only seen this once back in the 1990s," he said.', '"People are in for a shock, a lot of people are coming off 1.5% rates on fixed mortgages and they will be facing rates of five or six per cent.
