This would allow vessels to turn off their main engines while in port to reduce emissions and noise.', 'Eilidh Mactaggart, chief executive of the Scottish National Investment Bank, said the harbour project was "a critical platform for the transition in Scotland, delivering a high-specification facility which will shape industry in Aberdeen for decades to come".
