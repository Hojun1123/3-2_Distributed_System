The backlog dates to 2019.', 'Board papers showed 54 of the 61 outstanding investigations had exceeded the deadline.', 'NUH\'s director of midwifery Sharon Wallis told a board meeting in September: "We have a significant number of Serious Incidents. We have a cluster being reviewed.
