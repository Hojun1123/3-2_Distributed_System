', 'Italians will also be told to turn their heating down by one degree, and off for an extra hour a day. ', 'Some buildings will be exempt, including nurseries and hospitals.', 'The move comes as governments across Europe look to reduce demand and shore up energy supplies ahead of winter. ', 'Many were dependent on gas from Russia, which has been restricted following the war in Ukraine.
