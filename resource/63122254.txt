My struggle was always that I looked different, which was quite a hard thing to deal with," she says.', "But a photo - one she hadn't intended to upload at first because it showed her arm - started a journey towards her becoming a YouTuber and Instagram Reeler. ", '"I really liked the picture and couldn\'t redo it [to hide the arm].
