['<b class="ssrcss-hmf8ql-BoldText e5tfeyi3">A singing teacher said he was "gobsmacked" when Billie Eilish duetted with his TikTok tutorial to her song.</b>', 'Sheridan Coldstream, from Tingewick, Buckinghamshire, now has more than 163,000 followers on the platform.', 'Last month, the US singer-songwriter <a href="https://www.tiktok.com/@billieeilish/video/7115746885641538862" million views and 6.5 million likes.', 'Mr Coldstream said it was "completely astounding" that not only was Eilish singing to his piano playing, but the 20-year-old was also following his directions.', '"There\'s a bit in the song where she normally goes down," said the 58-year-old, who runs Total Vocal at his studio.', '"I suggested that some singers may want to take it higher and she
