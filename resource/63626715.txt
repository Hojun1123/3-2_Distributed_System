['<b class="ssrcss-hmf8ql-BoldText e5tfeyi3">An Army officer who made history trekking to the South Pole has begun a challenge to become the first woman to cross Antarctica alone and unsupported.</b>', 'Preet Chandi, known as "Polar Preet", aims to <a href="https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-derbyshire-62190718" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">travel more than 1,100 miles</a> across the frozen continent.', 'On Monday, she set off from Hercules Inlet and hopes to reach Reedy Glacier within 75 days.', 'The 33-year-old, from Derby, has already made history by trekking to the South Pole in 2021.', 'In "phase two" of her expedition, Capt Chandi, from Sinfin, set off on her coast-to-coast journey with her sledge, weighing about 120kg (264lb), loaded with her kit. ', 'She will have to battle temperatures as low as -50c and wind speeds of up to 60mph (96kmh). ', 'On her <a href="https://polarpreet.com/" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">blog</a>, she said it was "very windy outside but I\'m glad I\'ve started".', 'Last month, the <a href="https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-derbyshire-63407263" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Princess of Wales became the patron of the Army officer\'s goal</a> and wished her good luck in a phone call.', 'Capt Chandi said she hoped to "inspire others to believe in themselves and push their boundaries".', '"I was told no on many occasions, called stubborn or rebellious because I wanted to do things that were out of the norm and push my boundaries," she wrote.', '"Nothing is impossible. I\'ve always had this idea that I can achieve something great, something that allows me to be a role model. ', '"I want my 11-year-old niece to grow up without boundaries, knowing the possibilities of what you can achieve in life are endless."', 'Capt Chandi has previously spoken about how her challenge was "<a href="https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-derbyshire-59142164" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">considered out of the norm for an Asian woman</a>".', 'In January 2021, the trekker completed "phase one" and made history when she <a href="https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-derbyshire-59869042" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">became the first woman of colour to reach the South Pole</a> solo and unsupported.', 'She finished the route in 40 days, just short of the female world record of 38 days held by Joanna Davidson of Sweden.', 'Earlier this year, the Army officer <a href="https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-derbyshire-62050270" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">was made an honorary master</a> by the University of Derby.', 'The university wished her luck on her latest expedition and "all the best on this amazing endeavour".', 'Capt Chandi is a physiotherapist working at a Regional Rehabilitation Unit in Buckinghamshire, providing rehabilitation for injured soldiers and officers.', '<i class="ssrcss-xbdn93-ItalicText e5tfeyi2">Follow BBC East Midlands on </i><a href="https://www.facebook.com/bbceastmids/" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Facebook</a><i class="ssrcss-xbdn93-ItalicText e5tfeyi2">, on </i><a href="https://twitter.com/bbcemt" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Twitter</a><i class="ssrcss-xbdn93-ItalicText e5tfeyi2">, or on </i><a href="https://www.instagram.com/bbceastmidlands/" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">Instagram</a><i class="ssrcss-xbdn93-ItalicText e5tfeyi2">.
