', "Course bosses said the training, using state-of-the-art facilities, was preparing people to support the county's growing space industry.", 'The CSATT training, run by Truro and Penwith College, includes the use of facilities at its £7m specialist technology Valency centre in Truro.', 'The training comes as Spaceport Cornwall, based at Cornwall Airport Newquay, works towards its first launch of satellites into space from UK soil.', 'A Virgin Orbit plane and rocket <a href="https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/science-environment-63269429" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">arrived at Cornwall Airport Newquay in October</a>.', "Cornwall Council said the county's space sector had grown 164% since 2010 and was set to contribute £1bn to the local economy by 2030.
