', '"These increased costs are then compounded by a slowdown in trade and the fear of <a href="https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-devon-63543376" class="ssrcss-k17ofw-InlineLink e1no5rhv0">animal disease outbreaks</a>.
