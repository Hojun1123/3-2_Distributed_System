He was later arrested.', '"He came up to us and started arguing. He was speaking aggressively to us," Ms Harris told the court.', '"He hit me with his right hand into my face. It was with a clenched fist. ', '"It hit me on the cheek on the left side. I didn\'t go to the floor or anything but I was quite dizzy. I was in a daze."', 'Mr McAlary said it "was unbelievable that he would threaten to bottle someone over Covid".', '"He was holding his bottle like a baseball bat. He pushed Tess and then he punched her with a clenched fist. It was a bit of a haymaker. Then he turned and just ran off."', 'Beddow later told police he had become infuriated at Morrisons with people who were not obeying Covid restrictions.', 'He claimed a man and a woman had been calling him "fatty" and "fatbelly".
